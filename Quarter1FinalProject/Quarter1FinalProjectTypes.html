These are the games that I play:
Overwatch
Overwatch is a team based shooter game that is rated T for teen. It typically involves 2 teams of 6 players each battling each other for control of an onjective. THe game is played with a combination of heros who deal damage, heros who are more tanky, and heros who support and heal their allies. The only way to win in this game is by cooperating and communicating with your team.
Rocket League
Rocket League is an online sports game where you take control of a car, and try to hit a ball in a goal, much like soccer, but instead of people kicking a ball, you use cars. The typical game involves two teams of three people all trying to hit a ball into the opponents goal. It is a cooperative game, but it is less cooperative of a game than Overwatch.
Skyrim
Skyrim is a single player game where you take control of a warrior, theif, or mage back in medeival times, and you play through the main questline, gathering new items and strength as you go. It is entirely offline and single player, so your own skills, and your own gear determine if you win fights in this game.
