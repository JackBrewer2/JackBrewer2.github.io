These are some the important equations:
Velocity (V = X / T)
Often the building block for most of the premise of Physics, this equation has you divide the distance by the total time to give the velocity, assuming acceleration was constant throughout.
Force (F = M * A)
An easy to understand equation, Newton's second law regards force. To find it, just multiple the objects mass by its acceleration to get the force acting on it.
Energy (E = M * C^2)
The most famous equation in the world. This equation can be used to calculate energy as long as mass is understood, because the C is equal to the speed of light, which does not change.
